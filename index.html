About

I am the professor of Computer Science at the University of Glasgow, Scotland. I graduated from Edinburgh University in 2000 with a Bsc Computer Programming. I then went on to achieve my Phd Data Science from the University of Strathclyde. Honours graduates are eligible for membership of the British Computer Society and, after relevant work experience, they can apply to become full Chartered IT Professionals (CITP) and partial Chartered Scientists (CSci)/Chartered Engineers (CEng). MSci graduates are eligible for full CITP and partial CSci.
